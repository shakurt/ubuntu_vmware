# 🐧 Linux Basics

## 💻 What is Linux?

**Linux** is an open-source operating system based on the Unix architecture. Unlike Windows and macOS, it's free to use, customizable, and used in everything from servers and desktops to phones and embedded systems.

### 🔑 Key points:

- 🌟 **Open-source and community-driven**
- 🔒 **Secure and stable**
- 🎨 **Highly customizable**
- 🚀 **Used in servers, development, networking, and education**

---

## 📚 A Short History

- **1991** – 👨‍💻 **Linus Torvalds** created the Linux kernel as a hobby project
- 🔧 **Combined with GNU tools** to form a full operating system
- 🤝 **Over time**, developers and companies contributed to it
- 🌐 **Today** it powers the internet, supercomputers, Android devices, and IoT systems

---

## ⚖️ Linux vs Windows (Quick Comparison)

| 🏷️ **Feature**       | 🐧 **Linux**             | 🪟 **Windows**          |
| -------------------- | ------------------------ | ----------------------- |
| 💰 **License**       | Free & open-source       | Paid & closed-source    |
| 🎨 **Customization** | Very high                | Limited                 |
| 🔒 **Security**      | Very strong              | More vulnerable         |
| ⚡ **Performance**   | Lightweight & efficient  | Heavier resource usage  |
| 💻 **Terminal**      | Powerful & essential     | Optional & limited      |
| 🎯 **Usage**         | Servers, dev, networking | Desktop, gaming, office |

---

## 📦 What Are Linux Distributions (Distros)?

A **distribution** (or "distro") is a packaged version of Linux that includes the kernel plus desktop environment, tools, and apps. Different distros are designed for different purposes.

---

✅ Popular Linux Distros & What They’re Good For

### 🟣 **Ubuntu**

- 🆕 **Beginner-friendly**
- 💻 **Great for development and education**
- 👥 **Large community support**
- 🖥️ **Used in servers and desktops**

### 🔵 **Debian**

- 🏛️ **Very stable**
- 🏗️ **Used as the base for Ubuntu**
- 🖥️ **Great for servers**

### 🟢 **Fedora**

- ✨ **Cutting-edge features**
- 🏢 **Backed by Red Hat**
- 👨‍💻 **Mostly for developers and testing**

### 🔴 **Arch Linux**

- 🎛️ **Highly customizable**
- 🎓 **Targeted at advanced users**
- ⚙️ **Manual installation**

### ⚫ **Kali Linux**

- 🛡️ **For penetration testing & cybersecurity**
- 🔧 **Comes with security tools pre-installed**

### 🟡 **Linux Mint**

- 🔄 **Easy for Windows users**
- 🪶 **Lightweight and user-friendly**
- 🏠 **Good for personal use**
